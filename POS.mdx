### 1. Sales Page  
The Sales Page is designed for quick and efficient transactions. It allows your team to:
- **Browse Products**: Easily navigate through product using search bar.
- **Add to Cart**: Add multiple items to the cart with a single click.
- **Checkout**: Process payments swiftly with multiple payment options.
- **Print Receipts**: Instantly print receipts or send digital copies to customers.
- **Multiple Customer Support**: Sell to multiple customers at the same time using tabs, allowing you to switch between active transactions effortlessly.



### 2. Pricing Page  
The Pricing Page empowers your business to manage prices efficiently. It offers:
- **Price Management**: Set and update prices for all products.
- **Price Lists**: Create and manage different price lists for various customer groups.
- **Export Options**: Easily export your prices to XLSX or CSV formats, making it convenient to share or analyze pricing data.
- **Advanced Filtering**: Filter prices by Company, Price List, Value Rate, and Brand to quickly find and manage relevant pricing details.

## Why Choose Our POS App?

- **Seamless Integration with ERPNext**: All sales and pricing data syncs automatically with your ERP system.
