#### ðŸ”¹ Invoice List Section

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/recent-pos-invoice.png"
/>

- Displays a list of **recent POS invoices**.
- Includes:
  - Invoice number
  - Date and time
  - Customer name
  - Total amount
  - Payment status (e.g., paid, pending)
- You can **search** or **filter** the list by:
  - Invoice number
  - Date 
  - Payment status
- Clicking on an invoice will show **detailed information** for that specific transaction.

  - **View receipt**
  - **Print invoice**
