# 🏠 Home Screen

The **Home Screen** is the central interface of the POS (Point of Sale) system, designed for fast and intuitive sales operations.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/home-screen.png"
/>

## 🔍 Overview

The Home Screen is divided into two primary sections:

- **Left Panel :** Displays the current order summary.
- **Right Panel :** Shows the list of available items with a quick search and filter option.


<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/home-screen-1.png"
/>

---

## 🧾 Left Panel: Order Summary

- **Customer & Location Selectors:**  
  Drop-downs at the top allow selection of customer  (e.g., *Public*) and price list (e.g., *Public - Alger*).

- **Item Table:**  
  - Columns: `Item`, `Quantity`, and `Amount`.
  - Shows items added to the cart.

- **Keypad & Totals:**  
  - Numeric keypad for manual quantity/price input.
  - `Total Quantity` and `Grand Total` at the bottom.
  - Action buttons:
    - ✅ **Payment** — Finalize the order.
    - 🖨️ **Print** — Print receipt.
    - ❌ **Supprimer** — Remove an item.
    - ⬅️ **Retirer** — Undo last action.

---

## 🛒 Right Panel: Item Selector

- **Search Bar:**  
  Allows searching by item code, name, or barcode.

- **Item Cards:**  
  - Show item image (or placeholder), name, and price (e.g., `800 DA`).
  - Tapping adds item to the order.

---
## 🔄 Top-Right Buttons

Located in the top-right corner of the Home Screen, these buttons enhance user productivity and system connectivity:

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/home-screen-btns.png"
/>



### ✅ Sync Button

- **Label:** `Sync (1)`
- **Function:** Synchronizes local pos invoice with the central server.
- **Indicator:** The number in parentheses shows pending changes waiting to be synced.

> ⚠️ Important: Ensure stable internet before syncing to prevent data conflicts or loss.

---

### 2. 💰 Debt Button

- **Function:** Opens the **Debt Screen**, where users can view and manage all **outstanding customer debts**.

- (See the Debt Screen and Customer Debts sections in the documentation for more details.)

### 3. 🔄 Check-In / Check-Out Button

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/check_in_out_dialog.png"
/>


- **Label:** May vary (e.g., “Cash In/Out”)
- **Function:** Registers the flow of money **into** or **out of** the POS cash drawer.
- **Examples:**
  - 💵 **Cash In**: Register incoming funds, such as starting cash or deposits.
  - 💸 **Cash Out**: Record outgoing money, including expenses, withdrawals, or refunds unrelated to sales.

- **Use Case:** Improves accountability by tracking non-sale cash movements.



---

### 4. 📋 Menu Button



<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/pos-menu.png"
/>



- **Icon:** Three horizontal bars or lines
- **Function:** Opens a dropdown with advanced actions or settings.
#### Options May Include:

- **Recent POS Invoices**  
  - **What it does:** Displays a list of recent Point of Sale (POS) invoices for quick access or review.

- **Check In / Check Out**  
  - **What it does:** Allows you to check in or check out your POS session, tracking cash handling and register activity.

- **Unsettled Invoices**  
  - **What it does:** Shows any invoices that have not been fully Synced, allowing you to manage incomplete transactions.

- **Close the POS**  
  - **What it does:** Closes the POS system, finalizing any transactions, and locking out the register until reopened.

- **About**  
  - **What it does:** Displays information about the POS system, including version details and other relevant information.

---

These controls make it easy to manage both digital and cash-based transactions in a retail or service setting.
