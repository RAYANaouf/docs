## Creating an Opening Entry
1. Navigate to the **POS Opening Entry** module.
2. Enter the cash balance at the beginning of the day.
3. Save the entry.

## Managing Opening Entries
You can:
- View past opening entries.
- Adjust the balance if discrepancies are found.
