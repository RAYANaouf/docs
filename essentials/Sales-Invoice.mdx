## Creating a Sales Invoice
Sales invoices are created when products are sold on credit or as part of the payment terms:
1. Go to the **Sales Invoice** module.
2. Add the products or services sold.
3. Define the payment terms and due date.
4. Save and submit the invoice.

## Managing Sales Invoices
You can:
- Track paid and unpaid invoices.
- Apply payments or discounts.
- Email invoices to clients.
