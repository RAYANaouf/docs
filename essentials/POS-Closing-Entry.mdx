## Creating a Closing Entry
1. Go to the **POS Closing Entry** module.
2. Enter the cash balance at the end of the day.
3. Submit the entry.

## Managing Closing Entries
You can:
- View previous closing entries.
- Reconcile cash balances and adjust discrepancies.
