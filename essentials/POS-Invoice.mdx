## Creating a POS Invoice
1. Select the **POS Invoice** module.
2. Add items to the invoice by either scanning barcodes or manually selecting products.
3. Complete the payment, and the invoice will be finalized.

## Managing POS Invoices
You can:
- View existing POS invoices.
- Print or email invoices to customers.
- Track payment statuses.
